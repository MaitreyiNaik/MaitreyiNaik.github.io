What is it about?

It’s quite common for the early-twenties to get bored,
despite having a “smart phone” with them. So why not try something new?
Something like nail-biting incidents, short stories,
poems, photographs and a lot more?

Until a few days ago, I didn’t find enough courage
to put forth this website,
to share those small refreshing things
and to bring you back in an all excited and ebullient mood.
And now that I have done, I’d like to share something with you.
