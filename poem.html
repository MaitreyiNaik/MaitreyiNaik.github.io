Is it? Or, is it not?...

Date: 8th July, 2019

Gone is the time when you were there beside, each second.
Not always physically, but with no choice of second.
You loved my time and my company the most,
Showering your love and care on me, at the utmost.
Never had you left me unattended,
Never had I kept you waited.
Even if I did, did I not keep you informed?


The days when you first entered,
The days when we skedaddled,
The days when we talked,
The days when we waited,
"Where has that feeling gone which you once had, back in those days?"
When asked this question, "my love and respect for you, has and never will change", he says.
All of this happiness happened when we were beside,
And now, is filled with emptiness, when drifted out of sight.


"I'm in the office, surrounded with work and pressure"
"He is the office, surrounded with work, fun and people"
Correct me if I'm wrong,
Does someone get so busy for 9 complete hours that he can't even ping pong?
He has got bunch of seconds to share joy with his friends at office,
And a bundle of minutes to share my minutes with them.
Now tell me, does he not have a moment to message and take a break away from 'em?
Gives he lame excuses, that he has work piled up on,
But his friends, on the other hand, be with him through dusk and dawn.
For a good three months period, we were colleagues too,
Wouldn't I know how to work with the work with a shoo?


Saturation point is what it is called, that I waited till,
Then I had burst my emotions upon him with a heavy deep shrill.
I would say one; and instead of taking the topic to some other,
He would upsurge my anger, adding an against, another.
This would go on until I say, this tiff wouldn't bother,
Then would he pounce, to save the bond, rather.
The deep talks of convincing would all across bring chills,
For a good two days, leaving the mark of thrills.
The drama of ignorance begins on the third day,
If I feel it, should I not say?
If when shared something as a problem, should he not try to rectify it?
Instead, why does he amplify it?
Just like history repeats,
For him, his routine story never cheats.


His office, his work,
His friends and their twerk,
From the fraction of his 24 hours left,
I would want to commit his-leftover-time theft.
Why does he not see me craving for his attention?
Why does he only see me as an emotional tension?
He wants me to set him free.
Why should I? I am his girl. I am My-3.


The respect I seek,
Reflects through the time he peeks.
I'm vexed of the same old shit, repeating again and again,
Stepping into the barter of arguments, instead of the shoes of bargain.
Quoting in bold "you're wrong", you think you won?
Tell me what wrong have I done; is this not a better one?


Battle of emotions and swords of words,
Who would be at a loss, if we go off the chords?
Our dream, to dream together,
Our time, to spend together,
Our emotions, to share together
And your clothes, to wear together.
Should all of these go shattered?
Think again, if, to or not to, leave these words chattered.

BiZee Bee..

Date: 4th September, 2017

I was never the clinging type; I was rather with a let-go attitude,
But you changed my aptitude.
Though I'm not the same with others,
But all ways and always, will be the same with you.

I'm at that point of my life, where I need you the most
But you are nowhere to be found
In the matter of your own thoughts, you are completely entangled and immersed in.

When i stretched my hand, it was always left clinging.
Now I'm even tired to stretch, knowing it wouldn't find a strength to lean on.
This is not the life, but it goes on.
