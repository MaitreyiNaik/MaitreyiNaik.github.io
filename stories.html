Deluged with endearments to deluged with tears...

Shashi was blessed with geniality. She was a very jovial girl.
People would surround her, the moment she was found free.
She would make every-being happy, within moments.
Being an active participant in every field, she was the top-picked
student in the school. Everything happened in a sequel and
she prepared so very well for the exams that the upshot
of her whole efforts, was that, she was the topper of the school.
Days later, she joined one of the top colleges. It was then that
she started communicating with her friends through social media.
There was this guy, named Karthik, with whom she hardly spoke
during school days; he is now a very intimate friend of hers.
The relationship they shared started with friendship.
They grew so close to each other that the time and talk had no bounds.
He was very bewitched by her. Shashi beguiled him with her heart,
with her comely face, with her thoughts and with every asset that she held.


Karthik and Shashi were in different cities. It was almost two years and
they decided to meet. Both were onto making this meet a remarkable one,
for they were both all set, to manifest the signs of concern for each other,
to evince their love.
Shashi was even more excited to see him. Also, she was in a state of utter confusion.
Confusion drenched her mind, drowning her in many questions.
Her expressions went from confusion to excitement to back again.
The whole of her mind was occupied with the thoughts to satisfy him.
She couldn’t even make out the best outfit for her, from her closet.
After a quite good while, she picked up the finest clothes from her wardrobe,
which portrayed her subtle and elegant looks.
She was incomparable with any beauty found, as she redefined the beauty itself.
She made sure twice before she stepped out, that she'd taken the present to be presented to him.
As she stepped out of her house, her voluminous, jet-black hair met the morning light and the lustrous curls bounced as she walked. Her almond-eyes had its lashes and brows perfectly brushed.
Her bow-like lips were so appealing. She was ravishingly alluring.
In no time, she drove to Hyderabad Social and was waiting for him.


Karthik was equally excited to meet Shashi and he bought the
prettiest gift for her along with a bunch of red-roses.
He stepped out of his house being in a totally different world.
In the complete world of thoughts about Shashi.
Thoughts about how to please Shashi.
Just when he was thinking about this, he heard a harsh shrill, the screech of the vehicle.
People hurriedly gathered around. Karthik didn’t know what was happening.
He was not in a situation to move even his fingers.
He lay unconscious. Karthik felt extreme physical pain.
He then opened his eyes and saw a huge crowd all around.
Before he could even understand the scenario, a pool of blood flowed all across his body.
He felt the urge to contact Shashi. To tell her that he had set out to meet her.
To tell that he was bringing the thing that she likes the most.
To tell how much he loves her.
And finally, to apologize, for he was leaving her behind.
Karthik was now completely still. His heart stopped throbbing.
He was neither inhaling nor was he exhaling.
Every part in his body was at rest.
He was at rest.
And he was to rest in peace.


Shashi tried calling Karthik but to her surprise, there was no response.
It was the nineteenth call and there she was, almost shouting without even the hello-starts.
Police attended her call this time and narrated the whole of the incident.
She was totally baffled after what she heard.
She couldn’t even breathe.
She was completely broken into pieces.
Shashi somehow gathered a little courage and in a blink, she moved to the parking-lot.
Her hazel eyes were completely filled with tears.
Her heart was crying. Her mind was crying. Her soul was crying.
The complete she, was crying.
With every broken piece of her heart, she was trying to convince herself
that this was the wildest nightmare she had, but that was the bitter truth.
Days later, she went to his grave.
Shashi cried her heart out.
She kept the gift, that she bought for him, on his grave and then said aloud,
“I love you, Karthik. Rest in peace.”

Just a Jest...

He was exhilarated. His heart pounded with eagerness, with excitement.
A spark lit his heart and mind and he bounced from his bed,
when he heard the knock at the door, the sound he had been
prophesying since morning.
After being delayed for three days from its original arrival date,
the package finally hit his abode. Hari thwacked his book down
onto the floor and rushed to the door. He opened up the front door of the place.
He couldn’t help but to let a large smile or rather the widest grin
appear on his face when he looked down to see the perfectly wrapped carton,
containing the beast he was waiting all along- OnePlus 5,
which he had ordered just a couple of days ago.
The delivery man drove off before Hari could even wave him a goodbye,
so he simply scrooched down, before he picked the small-sized box
up off the ground, cradling it like a newborn as he brought it inside.
Hari held it so carefully and tight, yet gentle, as if clasping someone.
Closing the door, he sat on the chair, comforting himself to comfort the new-born.
He quickly grabbed the cutter that was readily kept on the table in-front,
to unwrap the package. His hands trembled with excitement
as he got closer to seeing that his new phone was indeed a physical one
and he wasn’t just dreaming.


When Hari was finally able to access the contents inside the bubble wrapped packaging,
he reached into the box and pulled out the actual OnePlus from the box and opened that up too.
It was just as beautiful as he had imagined it being,
and handled it with utmost care so that
he would not smudge the screen with his dirty fingerprints.
And now, he was all the way ready to turn the phone on and explore it to the fullest.
The screen lit up for a second, and it took a moment for it to go to its actual screen
on the display window where he needs to get it get started.
Upon finishing every step, Hari finally was there-accessing all the applications.
The very first thing that hit his mind was to get clicked.
So, ecstatically, he opened the camera app and got started.
It was at that instant that Hari noticed something freakish about the camera
that made his brows reach his hairline.
Hari tilted his head and examined it closely,
trying to scrutinize the thing that had caught his attention.
There were evidently, already clicked pictures on his supposedly “new” phone,
which he was wondering if it was simply repackaged.
Being the curious person that he is,
and due to the fact that he was now pissed over the fact that somehow
he had ended up with a used phone instead of the new one he had originally ordered,
Hari went to scroll through them.
When he did, he nearly dropped it,
his breath caught in the throat when he realized what he was seeing.
Chills ran down his spine and goosebumps formed
all along his arms, hands as he went over the photos.
The sight of them made his skin crawl.
He slowly gulped his drops of saliva down the throat. It was hard. Very difficult.
But somehow, gathered all his courage to view other snaps.
The pictures on his camera were the pictures of his very own house,
and more alarmingly, they were shot at night, from the woods in his backyard.
Hari couldn’t tell which one freaked him out the most - the one of him sleeping,
clicked from just beside his bed or
the one of him looking directly at the lens, sitting in the living room.
